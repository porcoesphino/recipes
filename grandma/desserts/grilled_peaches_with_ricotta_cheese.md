# Grilled Peaches With Ricotta Cheese

---

- *8* yellow peaches, halved with stones removed
- *2 tablespoons* sugar
- *2 tablespoons* maple syrup
- *2 teaspoons* butter
- *100 g* Ricotta Cheese
- *1 teaspoon* Vanilla paste or vanilla extract

---

Place peaches on a grill tray with cut side up. Pour a little syrup over each peach and fill with a little butter. Cook under a hot grill until bubbling and heated right through. Mix together ricotta, sugar and vanilla and serve with the grilled peaches.
