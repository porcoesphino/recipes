# Apple Strudel

---

- *1* Sheet Puff Pastry (or packet Puff Pastry â€“ roll thin and then cut in half)
- *700 g* apples
- *2 tablespoons* sugar
- *1 teaspoon* mixed spice
- *1 cup* sultanas
- *1 cup* milk
- *1* 300 ml carton cream
- *1* Vanilla Instant Pudding

---

1. Cut the sheet of pastry in half and cook for approx 10 mins or until golden brown. **Do not overcook.**
2. Peel, cut and cook the apples with sugar. Add spice and sultanas. Cool
3. Beat the milk and cream with the instant pudding until set.
4. Slice the pastry pieces in half across and fill with the pudding mix followed by the cooked apple.
5. Sprinkle Apple Strudel with Icing Sugar before serving.
