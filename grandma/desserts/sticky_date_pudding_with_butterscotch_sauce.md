# Sticky Date Pudding with Butterscotch Sauce

**8 Servings**

---

- *1 1/4 cups* (210g) seeded dates
- *1 1/4 cups* (310 ml) boiling water
- *1 teaspoon* bicarbonate of soda
- *60 g* butter, chopped
- *3/4 cup* (150g) firmly packed brown sugar
- *2* eggs
- *1 cup* (150g) self-raising flour

## Butterscotch

- *1 cup* (200 g) firmly packed brown sugar
- *300 ml* cream
- *200 g* butter

---

1. Grease deep 20 cm round cake pan, line base with baking paper.
2. Combine dates and water in pan; bring to boil then immediately remove from heat. Stir in the soda; allow to stand 5 minutes. Blend or process the date mixture with butter and sugar until almost smooth; add eggs and flour, blend or process until just combined. Pour mixture into prepared pan.
3. Bake in moderate oven about 55 minutes or until cooked through; cover with foil during baking if over-browning. Stand pudding 10 minutes before turning out of pan; serve with Butterscotch Sauce poured over the top.

## Butterscotch Sauce

Combine ingredients in medium pan; stir over low heat until sugar is dissolved and butter melted.

Sticky Date Pudding and Butterscotch Sauce can be made a day ahead. They can also be frozen very successfully.
