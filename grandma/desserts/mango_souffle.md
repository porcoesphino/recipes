# Mango Souffle

This was a recipe from Karen.

---

- *1* mango Jelly
- *300 ml* mango Puree
- *1 tablespoon* Sugar
- *1 tablespoon* Gelatine
- *1* small tin Evaporated Milk (chilled to almost frozen)
- *1* mango (peeled and diced)

---

1. Blend mango Puree, sugar and Gelatine and heat to dissolve Gelatine. Set aside to cool but not set.
2. Make Jelly as directed on packet. Pour into glass dish or individual serving glasses. Allow to set.
3. Slowly add mango Puree mixture to milk while still beating.
4. Finally, add diced mango and spoon through.
5. Pour onto set jelly. Decorate with a piece of mango or fine sprinkle of Chocolate Flake.
