# Rich Chocolate Self-Saucing Pudding

**6 Servings**

---

- *1 cup* self raising flour
- *3/4 cup* caster sugar
- *2 tablespoons* cocoa powder
- *1/2 cup* of milk
- *1 teaspoon* vanilla essence
- *30 g* butter, melted
- *3/4 cup* firmly packed brown sugar
- *1/4 cup* cocoa powder, extra
- *1 3/4 cups* hot water

---

1. Grease 2 litre (8-cup) ovenproof dish.
2. Sift flour, caster sugar and cocoa into large bowl; add the combined milk essence and butter, stir until smooth, pour mixture into dish.
3. Sift brown sugar and extra cocoa evenly over top.
4. Carefully pour hot water over pudding.
5. Bake in moderate oven for about 50 min or until pudding is firm. Dust with sifted icing sugar, if desired.
6. Best made just before serving.
