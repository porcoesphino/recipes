# Lemon Chiffon Pie

Great Grandma (Lil) Higgins made this on a regular basis when we had family gatherings.

---

## Biscuit Pastry

- *135 g* butter
- *1/2 cup* sugar
- *1* egg
- *1 1/2 cups* SR Flour
- *1 teaspoon* Vanilla Essence
- *1* pinch salt

## Lemon Filling

- *1* tin Condensed Milk
- *1/2 cup* Lemon Juice
- *2* eggs (yokes)

## Meringue topping

- *3* (up to 4) eggs (whites)
- *3 tablespoons* sugar
- *1 teaspoon* (small) vinegar
- *1 teaspoon* Vanilla

---

1. Pastry Cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla. Then add flour and salt. Press firmly into a flat baking dish. Cook in a Moderate oven for 15 minutes. Do not over bake.
2. Lemon Filling Put condensed milk into a basin and add beaten egg yolks followed by the lemon juice. Beat well and allow to set. Pour over cooled biscuit pastry base.
3. Meringue Beat egg whites with vanilla and vinegar until stiff. Gradually add sugar. Put onto the lemon mixture and return to cook in a moderate oven until light golden colour. This should take approximately 10 mins.
