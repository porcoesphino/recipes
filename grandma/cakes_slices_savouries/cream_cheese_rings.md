# Cream Cheese Rings

---

- *1 packet* puff pastry slices
- *2 packets* Philly Cheese
- *2 packets* French Onion Soup
- *2* eggs
- Extra cheese for topping
- Cayenne pepper

---

Combine Philly cheese, onion soup and eggs and spread over puff pastry. Roll and freeze. OR refrigerate for a little while and when firm again, cut into rings 2 cm thick. Place on an oven tray and grate small amount of cheese on top. Sprinkle with cayenne to taste. Cook in a moderate oven for 20 minutes or until golden brown.

## Alternate Fillings

Pastry sheets can be spread with Vegemite and cheese. Then roll and cut into 1 cm pieces before cooking.

A Savour chicken mince could also be used.
