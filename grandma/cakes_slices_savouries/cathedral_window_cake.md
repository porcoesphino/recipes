# Cathedral Window Cake

These cakes make a great Christmas gift. They are not cheap to make but could solve a gift problem. Try to use a high grade flour. This mixture makes 4 loaf shaped cakes.

---

- *1 kg* mixed glace fruits such as pears, pineapple, cherries and apricots
- *350 g* blanched almonds
- *650 g* Brazil nuts
- *9* eggs
- *1 1/2 cup* caster sugar
- *3 teaspoons* vanilla essence
- *1/2 cup* brandy
- *1 1/4 cups* flour
- *2 teaspoons* baking powder
- *2 teaspoons* mixed spice

---

Chop the glace fruits if large. Mix fruit, almonds and Brazil nuts together. In a large bowl beat eggs, caster sugar, vanilla essence and brandy together. Fold in sifted flour, baking powder and mixed spice into egg mixture. Mix fruit and nuts into egg mixture until combined. Divide mixture evenly between four baking paper â€“ lined 20 x 10 cm loaf tins. Bake at 160 degree C oven for 1 1/2 hours or until an inserted skewer comes out clean. Cool in tins. Remove lining paper and wrap in foil or seal in a plastic until ready to wrap for giving.
