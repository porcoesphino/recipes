# Date And Walnut Cake

From Great Grandma Higgins.

---

- *125 g* Butter
- *125 g* castor sugar
- *65 g* chopped dates and walnuts
- *1 dessertspoon* Honey
- *2 eggs*

- *1 cup* Plain flour
- *1 cup* grated carrot
- *1 teaspoon* cinnamon
- *1 teaspoon* baking powder
- *1/2 teaspoon* bicarb soda

---

Beat butter, sugar and honey. Then add the Dates and beaten eggs. Add flour and lastly fold in the grated carrot and walnuts.

Place in a loaf tin and bake in a moderate oven (170 degrees Fan Forced) for 30 mins. Check to see if cooked.
