# Pretzel And White Chocolate Treats

---

- *1 packet* of salted pretzels
- *3/4 cup* roasted peanuts
- *1 packet* melting White chocolate

---

Melt chocolate in a bowl over hot water being careful not to get any water in the chocolate mixture. When melted pour over the combined Pretzels and peanuts and coat well. Spoon into small piles onto a tray covered with baking paper and set aside to set. (Aim for 3 or 4 Pretzels each small pile.)
