# Peanut Cookies

From Vera Higgins. She often made these biscuits for us when I was sick some years back.

---

- *250 g* butter
- *125 g* brown sugar
- *180 g* white sugar
- *2 teaspoons* vanilla essence
- *2* eggs
- *430 g* Plain Flour
- *2 teaspoons* baking powder
- *1/2 teaspoon* bicarb soda
- *2 cups* peanuts

---

Cream butter and sugar. Add vanilla and eggs. Then add dry ingredients. Finally add the peanuts. Mix well together. Make into small balls and press down slightly onto a flat biscuit try.
