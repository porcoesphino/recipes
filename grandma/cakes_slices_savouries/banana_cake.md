# Banana Cake

This cake is gluten free and came from Nicole.

---

- *3* Bananas
- *3* eggs
- *3 cups* almond flour/meal
- *2 tablespoons* butter
- *2 tablespoons* honey or rice malt syrup
- *1 teaspoon* vanilla essence
- *1 teaspoon* baking soda
- *1/2 teaspoon* herbamere or sea salt - (herbamere is a healthy salt alternative)

---

1. Gently melt the honey and butter.
2. Combine almond meal, baking soda and salt in a bowl.
3. In a food processor blend bananas, eggs and vanilla.
4. Add melted honey and butter mixture and blend.
5. Finally add the dry mixed ingredients and blend further.
6. Pour into a well-buttered or lined loaf tin.
7. Cook in a moderate oven for 30 â€“ 40 minutes.
