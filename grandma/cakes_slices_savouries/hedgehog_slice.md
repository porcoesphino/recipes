# Hedgehog Slice

---

- *1* Packet Milk Arrowroot Biscuits
- *1 tablespoon* Cocoa
- *125 g* Butter
- *1* egg
- *120 g* sugar

## Icing

- *5 heaped tablespoons* icing sugar
- *1 small tablespoon* of cocoa
- *1 teaspoon* butter
- *1 teaspoon* vanilla essence and small amount of water to blend

---

1. Crush Biscuits and set aside in bowl ready for mixing
2. Add Cocoa to biscuit mix and stir through.
3. Prepare (line or grease flat Swiss roll tin)
4. Put butter, lightly beaten egg and sugar into a small saucepan and heat slowly stirring all the time.
5. When mixture thickened and just coming to boil, blend in with the crushed biscuit mix.
6. Press down firmly into tray and set aside to cool and ice with chocolate icing.

**Options**: Can add coffee or green colouring and mint flavouring to icing instead of cocoa. Can also add some walnuts or macadamia nuts to mixture.
