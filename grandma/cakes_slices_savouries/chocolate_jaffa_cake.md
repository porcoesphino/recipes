# Chocolate Jaffa Cake

From my special friend Val.

This is a very easy blend moist cake.

---

- *1* Orange cut into quarters. Remove Pith. 
- *170 g* Caster Sugar and pulse
- *3* eggs and pulse
- *170 g* butter or margarine
- *170 g* Self Raising Flour
- *170 g* Chocolate Chips

---

1. Pulse orange in a blender.
2. Add other ingredients and blend together but do not over pulse at this stage
3. Bake in a round hollow circle tin at 175 degrees for 30 minutes. Turn off oven and leave for a further 10 minutes.
