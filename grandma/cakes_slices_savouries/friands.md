# Friands

---

- *170 g* Butter
- *125 g* Almond Meal (or Hazelnut meal)
- *250 g* Icing Sugar
- *60 g* Plain Flour
- *5* egg whites

---

1. Beat egg whites until still. Melt butter. Carefully fold in the remaining ingredients.
2. Spoon mixture into patty papers or use a muffin tray.
3. Bake in a moderate oven (175 Fan Forced) for 15 â€“ 20 minutes.
4. Optional flavours can be added, such as blue berries, choc chips or raspberries.
