# Sponge Cake

Prepare 2 x 8inch round tins with baking paper.

---

- *4* eggs
- *3/4 cup* Castor Sugar
- *1/2 cup* Custard Powder
- *1/2 cup* cornflower
- *1 teaspoon* cream of tartar
- *1/2 teaspoon* bicarb soda

---

Sift custard powder and cornflour together with the cream of tartar and bicarb soda twice to ensure mixture blended through thoroughly.

Beat egg whites until stiff. Slowly add castor sugar. Then add the egg yolks but do not over beat at this stage. Slowly blend in the sifted flour using a metal spoon.

Pour quickly into the tins. Bake in a moderate oven 170 degrees C fan forced for 20 â€“ 25 minutes.
