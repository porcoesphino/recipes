# Date, Rice Bubble And Coconut Slice

---

- *1 cup* dates (cut in pieces)
- *150 g* (3/4 cup) brown sugar
- *95 g* butter
- *2 cups* rice bubbles
- *1 cup* rolled oats
- *1 cup* coconut

---

Boil together dates, sugar and butter. Combine with dry ingredients and press down firmly into a Swiss roll tin. Cool in refrigerator and cut into squares.
