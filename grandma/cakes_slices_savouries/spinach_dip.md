# Spinach Dip

---

- *1 packet* frozen spinach
- *1 cup* light sour cream (200 ml container)
- *1 cup* mayonnaise
- *1 packet* spring vegetable soup
- *1 tin* water chestnuts.
- *1 loaf* round bread for serving

---

1. Thaw and drain spinach.
2. Chop the water chestnuts into small pieces.
3. Cut a circle in the top of the bread loaf and scoop out bread. (This can be set aside for serving with the dip.)
4. Combine all ingredients and put into prepared bread bowl or in dip container. Serve with bread pieces or dry biscuits.
