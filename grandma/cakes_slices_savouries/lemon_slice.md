# Lemon Slice

From Karen Esplin.

---

- *1 packet* Arnotts Marie biscuits
- *125 g* melted butter
- *1/2 tin* condensed milk
- *3/4 cup* desiccated coconut
- *1* lemon

## Icing

- *2* cups icing sugar
- *1* squeeze lemon juice
- Soft butter to blend.

---

1. Grate lemon rind.
2. Crush biscuits and add to coconut and grated lemon rind.
3. Melt butter and add in condensed milk.
4. Add butter mixture to dry ingredients and mix well.
5. Line flat tin with baking paper for ease of removing slice to cut.
6. Press firmly into flat slice tin and set in the fridge.
7. When set, ice with a soft butter icing.
8. Cut slice into squares and store in an air tight container.
