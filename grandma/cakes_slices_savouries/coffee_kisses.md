# Coffee Kisses

Another of Great Grandma (Lil) Higgins regular recipes. She almost always had some on hard when guests called.

---

- *1 1/2 cups* SR Flour
- *125 g* butter
- *1* egg
- *1/2 cup* sugar
- *2 teaspoons* dissolved coffee

---

Beat sugar and butter to a cream. Add egg and beat well. Add flour and if a little stiff, some milk. Also add the coffee. Roll into small balls and press down onto a biscuit tray. Cook for 15 minutes in a moderate oven.

When cool ice with coffee icing and press together to make a kiss. Sprinkle with icing sugar.
