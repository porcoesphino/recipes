# Raspberry Shortbread

From Great Grandma (Lil) Higgins.

---

## Biscuit base

- *1 1/2 cups* Self Raising Flour
- *125 g* butter
- *1* egg
- *1/2 cup* sugar
- *1 teaspoon* vanilla essence
- Raspberry Jam

## Topping

- *1* egg
- *1/2 cup* sugar
- *1 large cup* coconut

---

Beat sugar and butter to a cream. Add egg and beat well. Add flour and if a little stiff, some milk. Also add the vanilla. Blend well together and press into a flat greased Swiss roll tin. This mixture will make 2 Swiss roll tins. Cook for 10 to 12 minutes in a moderate oven. Do not overcook.

To make topping, beat the egg, sugar and coconut together.

Spread with raspberry jam. Sprinkle over the topping. Bake in a moderate oven for 15 mins or until golden brown. Cut into slices.
