# Almond Bread

---

- *125 g* Plain Flour
- *125 g* Castor Sugar
- *2* eggs
- *125 g* whole almonds

---

Beat 1, 2, and 3 together. Add 4 and blend together.

Place in a date cake tin to bake in a moderate oven for approx 30 mins. Remove, cool and wrap. Keep in the fridge for at least 1 day.
