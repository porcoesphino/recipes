# Strawberry Liqueur Topping

---

- *1250 g* strawberries
- *3 cups* water
- *1 cup* port
- *1 cup* brandy
- *1* lemon (juice)
- *1* orange (juice)
- *10* strips orange
- *900 g* sugar

---

1. Cut strips of orange finely.
2. Combine water, sugar, orange, lemon and orange juice and boil for 15 minutes.
3. Add hulled strawberries and cook for 5 minutes.
4. Add Port and bring to boil.
5. Take off heat and add brandy.
6. Bottle when cooled a little into sterilised jars.
