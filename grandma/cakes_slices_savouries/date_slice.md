# Date Slice

---

- *240 g* Butter
- *250 g* brown sugar
- *1 cup* chopped dates
- *2 cups* mixed fruit
- *2 cups* Self Raising flour
- *2* eggs

---

Mix all ingredients together and push down firmly into a flat tray. Depending on your tray size this may make 2 trays. Bake in a moderate oven for around 30 minutes. Cool in tray and cut into slices when cold.
