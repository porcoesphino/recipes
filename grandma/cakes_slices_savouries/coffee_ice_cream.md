# Coffee Ice Cream

From my cousin Marilyn Hodgkins.

---

- *200 g* Philly Cream Cheese
- *1/2 cup* milk
- *2 heaped teaspoons* instant coffee
- *200 g* (3/4 cup sugar)
- *4 tablespoons* cream

- Extra whipped cream for decoration

---

1. Blend cheese, milk, coffee and sugar.
2. Pour into bowl and mix in 4 tablespoons cream.
3. Pour into wet mould and freeze.
4. When frozen, turn onto plate and decorate with whipped cream and nuts or chocolate flakes. Refreeze.
