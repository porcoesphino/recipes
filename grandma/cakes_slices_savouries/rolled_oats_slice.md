# Rolled Oats Slice

One of Grandadâ€™s Favourites.

---

- *2 cups* Rolled Oats
- *1/2 cup* of sugar
- *1/2 cup* coconut
- *1/2 cup* raw peanuts in skins
- *125 g* butter

---

Cut nuts into small pieces. Place all dry ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Melt butter and combine with dry ingredients. Press firmly into a flat greased tray (approx 26 cm x 18cm). Cook in a moderate oven for 20 mins or until golden brown. Allow to cool slightly and cut into squares. Do not remove from try until cold.

**Note**: This can become very brittle if over cooked.
