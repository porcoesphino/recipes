# Macaroons (No 2)

---

- *1* egg white
- *1/2 cup* sugar
- *3/4 tablespoon* melted butter
- *2/3 cup* rolled oats
- *1/2 cup* Coconut
- *1 drop* of vanilla essence

---

Beat egg white to very stiff. Gradually add sugar and vanilla. Add coconut and rolled oats. Mix well. Drop in teaspoonsful on greased paper. Bake 20 mins in slow oven.
