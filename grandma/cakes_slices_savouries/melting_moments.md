# Melting Moments (Also Known As Yo Yoâ€™S)

---

- *250 g* plain Flour
- *100 g* icing sugar
- *85 g* custard Powder
- *250 g* butter

## Icing

- *250 g* Icing Sugar
- *2 teaspoons* butter
- *1 tablespoon* boiling water
- Flavouring if desired

---

Cream together the butter and sugar and add the flour and custard powder previously well sifted together. Make into small balls about the size of a walnut, place on cold oven slide and press with a four pronged fork. Bake a till lightly coloured (approx 12/15 mins in a moderate oven). Allow to cool. Put two together with butter icing flavoured with vanilla. (add a touch of passionfruit if desired for additional flavour). Sprinkle with icing sugar. Store in an air tight container.
