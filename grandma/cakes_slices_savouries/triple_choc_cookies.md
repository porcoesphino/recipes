# Triple Choc Cookies

---

- *190 g* (1 1/4 cup) plain flour
- *40 g* (1/4 cup) Self Raising flour
- *50 g* (1/2 cup) cocoa
- *200 g* (1 cup) brown sugar
- *200 g* good quality dark chocolate
- *120 g* unsalted butter at room temperature
- *2* eggs
- *95 g* (1/2 cup) milk choc bits
- *95 g* (1/2 cup) white choc bits

---

1. Sift flours together.
2. Melt chocolate and butter in a medium sizes saucepan over low heat. Set aside 5 minutes to cool.
3. Add cooked chocolate mixture to eggs and dry ingredients. (Use a wooden spoon to combine).
4. Add extra choc bits.
5. Roll into balls and flatten each ball slightly with a fork.
6. Cook 15 â€“ 18 minutes in a medium oven.
