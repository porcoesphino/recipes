# Humming Bird Cake

- *3 cups* plain flour
- *1 teaspoon* bicarbonate of soda
- *1/2 teaspoon* salt
- *1 teaspoon* ground ginger
- *1 cup* sugar
- *1 1/2 cups* vegetable oil
- *3* x 60 g eggs, beaten lightly
- 225 tin crushed pineapple â€“ undrained
- *3* bananas mashed
- *1* cup chopped macadamia nuts (optional)
- *1* teaspoon vanilla essence

## Cream Cheese Frosting

- *60 g* unsalted butter
- *125 g* softened cream cheese
- *1/2 teaspoon* vanilla essence
- *1 cup* sifted icing sugar

---

Prepare 25 cm cake tin. Preheat oven to 180 C. Sift dry ingredients into a bowl. Make well in centre and add oil, slightly beaten eggs, pineapple, bananas, nuts (optional) and vanilla. Mix until combined. Pour into cake tin and bake for 1 hour 15 minutes or until cooked. Test with skewer in centre. Leave to cool. Turn out onto a cake rack and cover with cream cheese frosting.

Cream the butter and cream cheese with vanilla until soft and add the icing sugar. Spread frosting on cake and leave to set. Sprinkle with crushed nuts to garnish or rake with a fork for decoration.
