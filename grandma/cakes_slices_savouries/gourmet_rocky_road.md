# Gourmet Rocky Road

---

- *450 g* melted white eating chocolate. (Milk chocolate could be used as a substitute but this changes the unique flavour of this treat.)

## Chopped ingredients

- *300 g* Toasted marshmallows with coconut
- *400 g* Turkish delight
- *1/4 cup* (40g) toasted blanched almonds
- *1/2 cup* (75g) toasted shelled Pistachio nuts

---

1. Coarsely chop the chopped ingredients.
2. Combine all ingredients.
3. Put into two (2) 8cm x 26cm baking trays lined with baking paper. Cool in fridge. When set cut into 1 inch squares.
