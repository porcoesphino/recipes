# Mustard Sauce

From Pieta.

This is a great sauce to serve with cooked Silverside.

---

- *1* Egg
- *1/4 cup* sugar
- *1 tablespoon* flour
- *1 teaspoon* mustard
- *1 cup* liquid. (Liquid from cooked silverside is suitable)
- *1/4 cup* malt vinegar

---

Beat egg and sugar. Stir in flour and dry ingredients. Add liquid gradually stirring till thickened.
