# Vietnamese Salad

From Karen.

---

- *500 g* cooked, finely sliced chicken
- *2* sliced carrots
- *1/2* sliced cabbage
- *2* sticks sliced celery
- *1* finely sliced red capsicum
- *6* spring onions finely sliced
- *2 tablespoons* coriander
- *2 tablespoons* Vietnamese mint
- *3/4 cup* tablespoon roasted peanuts finely chopped

## Sauce

- *1* lime (juice)
- *2 tablespoons* Olive Oil
- *1 tablespoon* sugar
- *2 tablespoons* Sesame Oil
- *4 tablespoons* fish sauce
- *3 tablespoons* Soy Sauce
- *1 tablespoon* de seeded finely chopped chilli
- *4 tablespoons* Balsamic vinegar
- *4 tablespoon* of rice vinegar
- *2* cloves Garlic / Crushed Ginger

---

Place all dressing ingredients in a bowl and stir until sugar is dissolved. Mix all salad ingredients together in a bowl and add dressing just before serving.
