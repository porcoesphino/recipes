# Brown Rice And Nut Salad

---

- *1 cup* Brown Rice (Uncooked) - cook and cool
- *200 g* Currants
- *80 g* unsalted peanuts
- *80 g* pumpkin seeds
- *80 g* sunflower seeds
- *1/2* finely diced red capsicum
- *6* finely sliced spring onions
- (Finely shredded celery may be added if desired)

## Salad Dressing

- *2 tablespoons* olive oil
- Pinch of chilli powder (or use some fresh chilli finely chopped)
- *1 tablespoon* garlic wine vinegar
- *1 teaspoon* caster sugar
- *1 tablespoon* fresh coriander (cilantro).
- *1 tablespoon* Soy Sauce
- *2 tablespoons* Teriyaki Sauce
- *2* cloves garlic
- Similar quantity of fresh ginger
- *10 drops* sesame oil (or to taste)
- *1 tablespoon* balsamic vinegar
