# Chicken Chorizo Pilaf

---

- *4* cloves garlic Chorizo Sausages
- *2* onions
- *2* red capsicums
- Chicken Thighs
- *3* teaspoons Smoked Paprika
- *1 teaspoon* Chilli Flakes
- *1.3 litres* Chicken Stock
- *375 g* Rice
- *3 tablespoons* Parsley
- Lemon Juice

---

1. Brown chicken thighs. Take out and put aside.
2. Add crushed cloves and sliced vegetables. Add paprika, chilli flakes and Stock. Put ingredients in casserole dish. Return cooked chicken thighs to mixture. Add Salt. Sprinkle rice to mixture and place in 160 degree oven. Do Not Stir. Sprinkle with chopped Parsley and sprinkle with lemon juice.
