# Rissoles

---

- *500 g* mince
- *1* small finely diced onion
- *1* small grated carrot
- *1* small grated potato
- *1* small chilli diced
- *1* (up to 2) teaspoons finely grated fresh ginger
- *2* clove finely diced garlic
- *1* teaspoon (approx) mixed herbs or small quantity of fresh garden herbs
- *1* lightly beaten egg
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Seasoned flour

---

1. Mix all ingredients together and form into medium sized meatballs.
2. Roll in seasoned flour.
3. Gently fry in oil until golden brown.
