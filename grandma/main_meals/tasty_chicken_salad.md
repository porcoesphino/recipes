# Tasty Chicken Salad

- Packet of rice noodles, cooked
- Cooked chicken pieces,
- Spring onions, sliced
- Carrots, Julienned
- Red Peppers, diced
- Snow Peas
- Bean sprouts
- Coriander chopped

## Dressing

- Soy sauce
- vinegar
- lime juice
- garlic
- ginger
- red chilli

---

1. Cook chicken pieces and set aside. Also cook rice noodles and set aside.
2. Add the carrots, red peppers, snow peas to pan and lightly cook. Then add the cooked chicken, bean sprouts and coriander.
3. Combine the noodles with the chicken and vegetable mix and pour over prepared sauce before serving.
