# East West Lamb

This will serve 4 people

(One of the recipes used at the B and B in Nornalup)

---

- *12* Lamb cutlets
- *2* orange sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 2.5 cm dices
- *20 ml* sesame oil
- *2* red chilli peppers, finely sliced with the seeds intact
- *3* thumbs ginger root julienned
- *200 ml* boiling water
- *15 g* coriander leaves
- Salt and pepper
