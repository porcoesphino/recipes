# Sausage Rumble And Mash

---

- Diced bacon
- Sliced Onion
- Diced Savoy Cabbage
- Potatoes
- Swedes
- Sausages (Chicken/Pork/Beef)
- Grated Tasty Cheese

---

1. Peel, dice and boil potato and Swede together. When cooked mash with butter, salt and pepper.
2. Fry bacon, onion and add Savoy cabbage.
3. Stir into mashed potato and Swede mix.
4. Place cooked ingredients into a casserole dish and cover with grated cheese. Brown in oven approx 20 mins.
5. Cook the sausages and serve with spoonful of mash. Garnish with lemon or parsley
