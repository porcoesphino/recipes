# Fennel Salad With Citrus Zest And Toasted Pine Nuts

There are 2 versions of this recipe here. Both of them are great recipes to serve with fish.

**4 Servings**

---

- *1* large Fennel bulb
- *1* Lemon zest and juice
- *1* Orange Zest and segments
- *3 tablespoons* toasted Pine Nuts
- Olive Oil to taste
- Sea Salt flakes
- freshly ground Pepper

---

1. Cut the shoots from the fennel and discard, reserving a good handful of green fronds.
2. Chop these and toss them into a bowl.
3. Peel the fennel bulbs, halve, core, and slice paper-thin, using a mandolin if you have one.
4. Add to the bowl.
5. Grate over the zests. It is good to add some Orange Segments for added flavour.
6. Squeeze lemon juice over, to taste.
7. Add the pine nuts.
8. Drizzle over oil, to taste.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Toss, taste and adjust the seasonings, and serve immediately.
