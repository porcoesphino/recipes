# Sweet Potato Fritters With Chicken Breasts

---

- *600 g* chicken breast fillets, skin removed
- *2 tablespoons* lemon juice
- *600 g* orange sweet potato, peeled and grated
- *5* shallots, finely chopped
- *2 teaspoons* minced ginger
- *1 teaspoon* minced garlic
- *3* eggs, lightly beaten
- *1/3 cup* flour
- *1/2 cup* sweet chilli sauce
- *4 tablespoons* coriander leaves
- *1* lemon (juice and rind)

---

Place chicken breasts into pan and cook five minutes each side. Pour over the lemon juice during the last minute of cooking. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil and keep warm while you make the fitters.

Place the grated sweet potato, shallots, ginger, garlic, flour, beaten egg into a bowl and mix well. Spoon onto lightly oiled pan and cook over low heat for 4 minutes on each side or until golden and cooked. Remove from the pan and keep warm while cooking the remaining fitters.

Serve the fitters onto plates. Cut the chicken into slices and place on top of the fritters. Spoon over the sweet chilli sauce and scatter with coriander.
