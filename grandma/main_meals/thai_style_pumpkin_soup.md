# Thai Style Pumpkin Soup

---

- *750g* (up to 1kg) pumpkin, peeled & cubed
- *3 cups* chicken stock
- *1* x 150gm can coconut cream
- *2 teaspoons* Thai fish sauce
- Cooked prawns or chicken breast to serve
- Coriander for garnish

## Fried ingredients

- *2* tablespoons peanut oil (or 3)
- *1* medium onion, chopped
- *2* cloves garlic
- *1 teaspoon* fresh ginger, grated
- *2 teaspoons* (up to 4) Thai red curry paste
- *1* stalk lemon grass, bruised

---

1. Heat oil and add fried ingredients and stir fry them.
2. Add pumpkin, lemon grass and stock, simmer until tender.
3. Remove lemon grass.
4. Blend with hand blender.
5. Add coconut cream and reheat.
6. Add fish sauce to taste.
7. Pour over prawns or chicken.
8. Garnish with coriander.
