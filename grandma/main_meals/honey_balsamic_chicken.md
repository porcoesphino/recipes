# Honey Balsamic Chicken

---

- *1/4 cup* balsamic vinegar
- *6 tablespoons* honey (a little less if preferred)
- *2* or 3 tablespoons olive oil (divided in half) TODO:
- *2* cloves minced garlic
- *1 teaspoon* dried thyme
- *3/4 teaspoon* dried Rosemary
- *1/2 teaspoon* Red Chilli flakes
- *3/4 teaspoon* Kosher salt
- *1/4 teaspoon* black pepper
- *2 lbs* chicken breasts
- *1 1/2 lbs* baby red potatoes – quartered
- *1* punnet of cherry tomatoes
- *1 lb* green beans – trimmed (can use asparagus as an alternative)

---

1. Pre heat oven to 175 C.
2. Mix balsamic vinegar, honey, 1 tablespoon olive oil, garlic, thyme, rosemary, chilli, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl and stir well.
3. Place chicken breasts in a re-sealable plastic bag and moor in the marinade. Seal and toss chicken to evenly coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
4. While chicken marinates, place the potatoes and tomatoes on a flat baking tray. Drizzle with the remaining o9il. Season with Salt and pepper. Coat well and bake for 25 minutes or until cooked.
5. Remove tray from the oven and toss in the green beans.
6. Remove marinated chicken breasts and place on the vegetable mix. Then pour over the remaining marinade.
7. Return tray to oven and bake 30 – 40 minutes occasionally basting the chicken until the internal temperature of chicken is 74 degrees is you have a cooking thermometer.

Rest 5 – 10 minutes before serving.
