# Seafood Pineapple Cocktail

---

- *1 kg* cooked prawns – peeled and de veined
- *2* x small tins pineapple pieces – drained. Keep 1/4 juice
- *1* large tin red salmon – drained. (Can use tuna)
- *2* red peppers – finely sliced
- *1 cup* water cress leaves
- *10* lettuce leaves for serving
- *1/2 cup* chopped parsley

## Pineapple Sauce

- *1/4 cup* pineapple juice
- *1/2 cup* mayonnaise
- *2 tablespoon* cream
- *2 tablespoons* tomato sauce

---

1. Combine all ingredients to make the sauce.
2. Combine all together and place into lettuce leaf. Sprinkle with parsley and pour sauce over before serving.
