# Fruity Coleslaw

Canadian dish

---

- *2 cups* finely chopped cabbage
- *2* finely diced red apples
- *1/4 cup* chopped walnuts.

## Dressing

- *1/2 teaspoon* paprika
- *2 tablespoons* milk
- *1 teaspoon* white sugar
- *1/2 cup* mayonnaise

---

Whisk dressing ingredients. Fork dressing through coleslaw dish. Serve chilled.
