# Sweet Potato And Lentil Soup

Can substitute Parsnip for the sweet potato for a lighter flavour

---

- *2* brown onions peeled and chopped
- *1 tablespoon* butter
- *2* garlic cloves crushed
- *2* sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped
- *1 teaspoon* turmeric
- *2 teaspoons* chopped chilli
- Pinch nutmeg
- *2 teaspoons* ground coriander
- *125 g* red lentils
- *1.5 litres* chicken stock
- Salt and pepper

---

Gently fry the onion in butter until it softens. Add the garlic and cook a further three minutes. Add the sweet potato and spices and toss. Put in the lentils and stock and simmer gently for 35 minutes. Puree with a wand or cool, and then pour into a food processor and puree. Season to taste.
