# Tuna Bake

---

- *1* x 425g can tuna
- *1* large jar tomato puree or Bolognaise sauce
- *1* small onion (chopped)
- *1 cup* green peas and corn
- *1/2* packet Pasta noodles
- Grated Cheese
- Breadcrumbs
- Several teaspoons butter

---

1. Boil pasta for 12 minutes in salted water with a little oil added. Strain and set aside.
2. Combine all ingredients and place in a casserole dish. Cover with grated cheese and small quantity of breadcrumbs. Add several teaspoons butter to help form crust.
3. Bake in a moderate oven for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
