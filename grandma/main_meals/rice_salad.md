# Rice Salad

From my Friend Val Blakemore.

---

- *1 cup* uncooked white rice. Cook and set aside to cool
- *1/4 cup* vinegar
- *1/4 cup* olive oil
- *2 teaspoons* curry powder
- *2 tablespoons* brown sugar
- *1* tin whole corn
- *1/2* finely cut onion
- *1/2* green pepper (capsicum)
- *3* stalks celery
- *1/4 cup* raisins
- *1/4 cup* salted peanuts

---

Combine all ingredients.
