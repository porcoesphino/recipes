# Mixed Vegetable And Lentil Curry

Per serve, 20 g fat (1847kJ).

**6 Servings**

---

- *2 tablespoons* peanut oil
- *2* large brown onions (400g) chopped coarsely
- *2* cloves garlic, crushed
- *1 tablespoon* grated fresh ginger
- *2 tablespoons* red curry paste
- *1.5 litres* (6 cups) vegetable stock
- *2 cups* (400g) red lentils
- *4* medium carrots (480g) chopped coarsely
- *250 g* baby green beans
- *500 g* cauliflower florets
- *1 cup* (250ml) coconut milk
- *1/4 cup* coarsely chopped fresh coriander leaves

---

1. Heat oil in large saucepan; cook onion, stirring, about 5 minutes or until very soft. Add garlic, ginger and paste; cook, stirring, until fragrant.
2. Add stock and lentils; bring to boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered 5 minutes. Add carrot, beans and cauliflower; simmer, uncovered about 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
3. Add coconut milk and coriander; cook stirring, until heated through.

Serve with steamed rice, cucumber raita and chilli-flavoured pappadums.
