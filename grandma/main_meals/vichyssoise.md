# Vichyssoise (Potato And Leak Soup)

This dish was from the International Cooking Class and came in the French section.

---

- *4* Leaks
- *80 g* butter
- *4* small potatoes
- *1* teaspoon salt
- *1* pinch of cayenne pepper
- *1 litre* chicken broth
- *150 ml* pint cream
- *1 1/2 tablespoons* milk
- Chopped chives to garnish

---

Prepare leaks by removing the outer leaves and wash. Cut them into smallish rounds. Melt the butter in a large saucepan and add sliced leaks and fry until lightly cooked (saut√©). Add the chicken stock, salt and pepper. Peel and cut potatoes into small pieces and add to the soup mix. Leave to simmer for about 1 hour. Place the soup through a sieve and add cream and tablespoons milk before serving. Do not re boil.
