# Pumpkin And Chickpea Salad

---

- *1* tin Chickpeas (drain) (or 1 cup uncooked)
- *250 g* Pumpkin
- *100 g* Olives
- Parmesan Cheese slices or Feta Cheese

## Dressing

- *1* small diced chilli
- *1* clove garlic
- *2 cm* fresh grated ginger
- *100 g* Sun dried tomatoes
- *3 tablespoon* balsamic vinegar
- *1 tablespoon* teriyaki sauce
- *1 tablespoon* light soy sauce
- Salt and pepper to taste

---

1. Cook chickpeas. Soak in water for 1 hour. Cover and boil for 1 hour or until soft.
2. Dice pumpkin into 1 1/2 cm cubes and fry lightly in oil or spray with oil and bake in the oven for 15 minutes or until soft.
3. In Frypan, combine pumpkin cubes, chickpeas, chilli, garlic and olives. Make dressing of garlic, ginger, salt, pepper, Soy sauce, Balsamic Vinegar, Teriyaki sauce and sundried tomatoes. Stir through mixture before serving.
