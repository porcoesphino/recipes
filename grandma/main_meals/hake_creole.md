# Hake Creole

---

- *6* Hake Fillets (or substitute another white Fish)
- *1 tablespoon* Chilli Sauce
- *3* large tomatoes (diced)
- *2 cups* chicken stock
- *1 1/2 tablespoon* Flour
- *1 1/2 tablespoon* Butter
- *1 tablespoon* Vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- *1 teaspoon* Anchovy sauce
- *1 teaspoon* paprika
- *1/2 teaspoon* cloves
- *1/2 teaspoon* rosemary

---

1. Cover fish with vinegar, salt and pepper and dot with butter. Cook slightly using a frypan.
2. Make sauce by combining butter and flour in a saucepan and heat together cooking for about 2 minutes.
3. Slowly add the Chicken stock followed by the chopped tomatoes.
4. Add the chilli and anchovy sauces, paprika, cloves and rosemary.
5. Combine all ingredients and place into a flat casserole dish and finish cooking in the oven for approx 15 minutes.
