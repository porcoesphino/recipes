# Tuna Mornay

---

- *1* x 425g can tuna
- *1 cup* soft breadcrumbs
- *60 g* butter
- *1* large onion (chopped)
- *4 cups* milk
- *2 tablespoons* plain flour
- *3* chopped hard boiled eggs
- *1* lemon (juice)
- *1 cup* grated cheese
- Seasoning to taste

---

Saut√© onion in hot butter till transparent. Remove from heat and stir in flour, then milk. Return to heat and stir until sauce thickens. Add all other ingredients reserving half the cheese. Place in casserole dish. Top with grated cheese. Bake in a moderate oven 15 minutes or until golden brown.
