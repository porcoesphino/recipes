# Burmese Chicken Curry

From Thornlie Home-Makers.

---

- *1 lb* egg noodles
- *1 kg* chicken pieces or thigh meat
- *2* large brown onions
- *4 tablespoons* oil
- *3 tablespoons* desiccated coconut
- *3 tablespoons* peas-meal (ground yellow dried pea flour)
- *2 teaspoons* salt
- *2 teaspoons* turmeric
- *1/2 teaspoon* ground ginger
- *1/2 teaspoon* cayenne pepper
- *2 cloves* garlic
- *5 cups* water

## Garnish

- *1* raw white onion
- *1* or 2 lemons (juice)

---

1. Cook egg noodles as per instructions on the packet
2. Cut chicken into very small pieces after it is cooked and boned. Mix peas meal in 1 cup of water and leave aside for thickening.
3. Slice onions and garlic and fry in oil until golden brown. Add chicken, coconut, cayenne, turmeric, salt and ginger. Cook 15 minutes.
4. Add 6 cups of water. Also add peas-meal mixture. Cook on strong heat for 30 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent lumps forming. Simmer 15 minutes.
5. Serve noodles in a bowl with a good serve of Chicken Curry. Garnish with lemon juice and thin slices of raw white onion.
