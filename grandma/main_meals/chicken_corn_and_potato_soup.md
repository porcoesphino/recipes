# Chicken, Corn And Potato Soup

---

- *500 g* diced chicken breasts
- *4* medium potatoes
- *2* sticks of celery
- *1* onion
- *1 teaspoon* salt
- Freshly ground pepper
- *1 litre* chicken stock (made with Massel stock cubes)
- *1 level tablespoon* butter or margarine
- Chopped fresh coriander (optional)

---

1. Peel potatoes and onion. Wash celery. Cut all vegetables into cubes. Melt butter in saucepan, add the vegetables and put lid on. Cook without browning for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add chicken and cook until white.
2. Add stock, salt and pepper and simmer covered for about 30 minutes.
3. Serve in large bowls and sprinkle with chopped coriander.
