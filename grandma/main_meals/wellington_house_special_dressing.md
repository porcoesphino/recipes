# Wellington House Special Dressing

This recipe was created by Naoyo and regularly used on salads when we ran the B & B at Nornalup. It was always commented on as being extra tasty.

---

- *2 teaspoons* Sesame Oil
- *1 teaspoon* fresh grated ginger
- *1* clove crushed garlic
- *1 1/2 tablespoons* Soy Sauce
- *3 tablespoons* lemon juice
- *4 tablespoons* White vinegar
- *1 tablespoon* Balsamic Vinegar
- *1* small finely diced Chilli
- *1 tablespoons* finely sliced Coriander Stems and Roots. (Also can substitute finely sliced Rocket Stems for added flavour)

## Initial ingredients

- *3 tablespoons* Light Olive Oil
- *2 teaspoons* brown sugar or palm sugar
- *1/2 teaspoon* pepper
- *1/4 teaspoon* salt

## Optional

- *1 teaspoon* lightly browned Sesame seeds

---

Blend the initial ingredients together well. Add the remaining ingredients.
